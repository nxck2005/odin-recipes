Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is an Italian-American dish featuring pasta tossed in a creamy Alfredo sauce. with sauteed or grilled chicken as a topping.

The sauce is usually made with butter, Parmesan cheese, and cream. It has a rich and decadent flavor.

Ingredients

  • For the noodles:
    • Dry fettuccine pasta
  • For the chicken:
    • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    • Italian seasoning
    • Kosher salt
    • Pepper
    • Extra-virgin olive oil
    • Butter
  • For the sauce:
    • Butter, cut into large cubes or slices
    • Heavy whipping cream
    • Minced garlic
    • Garlic powder
    • Italian seasoning
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water.
  2. Add the pasta and cook as the package describes, usually 10 minutes.
  3. Reserve 1/2 of the cooking water, drain well, and set aside.
  4. Season the chicken breasts with Italian seasoning, salt and pepper.
  5. Warm the olive oil over medium-high heat in a large nonstick skillet.
  6. Once it't shimmering, swirl the pan to evenly distribute.
  7. Add the chicken and leave undisturbed for 5-7 minutes.
  8. When the bottom is golden brown, flip over and add in 1 tbsp of butter between them, and pick up the pan and give it a gentle swirl.
  9. Continue cooking for another 5-7 minutes, or until internal temperature reaches 165F.
  10. Transfer chicken to a cutting board and let rest for 3 minutes.
  11. Cut into 1/2-inch thick slices.
  12. Tent with foil while you prepare the sauce.
  13. In the same pan, over medium-low heat, add butter and cream.
  14. Whisk until butter has melted.
  15. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook for 3-4 minutes. Do not boil!
  16. Whisk constantly while you do, until it starts to thicken.
  17. Stir in the Parmesan cheese just until melted and the sauce is smooth.
  18. If sauce is too thick, add the reserved pasta cooking water a few tablespoons at a time to thin it out.
  19. Take sauce off the heat and immediately toss with the cooked fettuccine noodles.
  20. Done! Garnish with parsley, more Parmesan, and black pepper if desired, and serve.
