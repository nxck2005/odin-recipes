Honey Chilli Potato

Description

Honey Chilli Potato is a great and popular Chinese dish that is usually served as an appetizer or with a main dish. It has a savory, spicy flavour but a hint of sweetness.

It mainly has crispy potato fingers that are made with various sauces and then topped off with honey.

Home

Ingredients

  • For potatoes:
    • 2-4 medium sized potatoes
    • Cornstarch for coating and normal+cornflour
    • Salt, red chilli powder+paste for seasoning
    • Oil for frying
  • For the sauce:
    • Oil for sauteing
    • Finely chopped or minced garlic
    • Sliced capsicum
    • Soy sauce
    • Chilli sauce
    • Tomato ketchup
    • Honey
    • Sesame seeds for garnishing

Steps

  1. Mix together all ingredients for the dry batter: chilli powder + paste, garlic paste, all purpose flour, cornflour and salt.
  2. Add potatoes and coat in dry batter.
  3. Deep fry the potatoes in batches and drop them one at a time so they dont stick!
  4. Deep fry on a medium high flame until theyre golden brown.
  5. Remove and set aside.
  6. Make a thick batter by mixing all purpose + corn flour, pepper and water.
  7. Dip each half fried potato stick in it.
  8. Fry the coated potatoes, on high heat, one at a time.
  9. Fry till golden brown, remove on tissue paper and set aside.
  10. For the sauce, heat oil in a large wok and add garlic. Saute for 30 seconds, don't let it brown.
  11. Add sesame seeds and saute for another 30 seconds, again being careful not to brown.
  12. Add ketchup, honey, chilli paste, chilli flakes, soy sauce and water, and vinegar according to taste.
  13. Add the potatoes set aside earlier, and toss well until they're coated.
  14. Add cornflour slurry, and toss again for two minutes till the cornflour cooks through.
  15. You have yourself a great appetizer!
